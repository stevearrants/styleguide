    Address your user

    Content from Technology often asks the reader to do something or take some action Use the second person singular whenever you can.

    You can get training to use the Element Editor.

    Find out how your JIRA Service Desk account works.

    If you create content for multiple users, say a group leader and their group, address the primary user as you and refer to the other users by their role or titles.

    You can adjust the settings for Production Specialists in the Admin panel.
